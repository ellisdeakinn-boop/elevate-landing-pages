The mechanism

Four steps.
Same process.
Every single day.

01
Establish your daily bias on the higher timeframe
Check the daily chart. Is the market making higher highs and higher lows, or lower highs and lower lows? That's your direction for the day. You're only looking for trades that align with it — nothing else gets considered.
02
Identify key levels — points of interest
Drop to the lower timeframe and mark the levels where price is likely to react: previous swing highs/lows, fair value gaps, liquidity pools. Set alerts. Walk away. You don't sit at a screen watching every candle.
03
Wait for confirmation. Then execute.
When price reaches your level, you wait for the confirmation signal — a structure shift on the lower timeframe, a defined candle pattern. No confirmation: no trade. Entry, stop, and target are set before you click the button.
04
1% risk per trade. Minimum 1:2 reward. Log everything.
Position size is calculated every time — never by feel. If the trade hits stop: 1% loss, move on. If it hits target: 2%+ gain. Over 100 trades, this maths is undefeated. The journal shows you where to improve.
