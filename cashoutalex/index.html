Why Most E-Commerce Stores Fail in the First 90 Days — and How the 7-Figure Students Avoid It

01
Wrong Product Selection Process
Starting with a product you like instead of a product the data proves the market wants. Product research isn't about finding something unique — it's about finding something proven, then making it better or cheaper or faster.
02
Unprofitable Ad Spend
Running ads without understanding unit economics. If your cost of goods, shipping, and ad spend combine to more than your AOV, you lose on every sale regardless of your conversion rate. The math has to work before you scale.
03
No Email Revenue
Paid traffic builds the list. Email monetises it. Most stores leaving money on the table aren't spending too much on ads — they have zero backend. 30 to 40% of e-commerce revenue should come from email. Most stores are at 0.
04
Scaling Before Optimising
Turning up ad spend on a store that converts at 1.2% doesn't make money — it loses it faster. Every dollar of ad spend is wasted if the foundation is not optimised first.
