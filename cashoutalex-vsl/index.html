Why Most FBA Sellers Fail in the First 90 Days

01
Wrong Product Selection
Starting with a product you like instead of what data proves the market already wants. Product research isn’t about finding something unique — it’s about finding something proven, then making it better, cheaper, or faster.
02
Broken Unit Economics
Running ads without understanding your numbers. If COGS + FBA fees + ad spend exceed your selling price, you lose on every unit regardless of volume. The math has to work before you scale.
03
No Review or Reorder System
Most FBA sellers leave 30–40% of potential revenue on the table by ignoring review follow-up, reorder sequences, and product bundles. One product isn’t a business — a system is.
04
Scaling Before Optimising
Turning up ad spend on a listing converting at 1.2% doesn’t make money — it loses it faster. Every variable has to be dialled in before spend goes up.
