What I Found

Four gaps I found in Legacy Trading Academy's marketing

  • Too many steps in the VSL funnel — each extra page between the ad and the sale is a drop-off point. Most people will not make it through a multi-step flow, they will just leave
  • Free community option on the front end is cannibalising paid conversions — when given a free path, most people take it. That kills your cash collected before they ever reach the paid offer
  • Headline is not optimised for a biz-op audience — "learn how to trade" attracts hobbyists. The buyers in this space are motivated by income replacement and leaving their job, not learning a skill
  • No retargeting pixel — everyone who hits the funnel and leaves is gone permanently. That warm traffic is being wasted every day
