The opportunity

The biggest marketplace
in the world.
Most of it is untapped.

Amazon has over 300 million active customers and processes more than $500 billion in annual sales. The infrastructure — storage, fulfilment, payments, customer service — is already built. You just need to put the right products in front of the right buyers.

Online arbitrage is the simplest entry point into this market. You're not manufacturing anything. You're not building a brand from scratch. You're finding products that are already selling at a lower price somewhere else, and listing them on Amazon at the market rate.

The margin is the business. The platform does the rest.

The mechanism

Four steps.
Repeat until
$10K/month.

01
Source products with the right margin
Use sourcing tools to find products available below Amazon's selling price. Apply the margin formula — buy price + Amazon fees vs. current sell price. Only proceed if the net profit clears 30%+.
02
Send inventory to a prep centre
Products go to a third-party prep centre — they inspect, label, and ship to Amazon's fulfilment network. You never touch the stock. From purchase to live listing: typically 5–10 days.
03
Win the buy box. Get sales.
Repricing software automatically adjusts your prices to win the buy box without destroying your margin. Amazon handles the sale, payment, packing, shipping, and customer service.
04
Reinvest. Scale the product catalogue.
Profit goes back into more inventory across more products. $10K/month in this model isn't one viral product — it's 30–50 consistent products each doing modest, predictable volume.
